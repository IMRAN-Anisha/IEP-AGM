02 Concept Evaluation

Three directions, weighed against
feasibility and innovation.

With our user needs and design requirements established, we generated three distinct concept directions and assessed each one for feasibility, innovation and fit against the constraints of remote, off-grid operation on Lama Lama Country. Two evaluation tools — a feasibility-vs-innovation map and a weighted decision matrix — helped us compare options side by side and select a direction with confidence.

01 Concept A

Predictive Maintenance Analytics System

  • Monitors energy equipment to identify patterns.
  • Analyses collected data to predict when faults will occur and when maintenance is required.
02 Concept B · Selected

SMS / Low-Bandwidth Alert System

  • Sends simple notifications in the form of a text message when faults or unusual activity is detected.
  • Can function with limited internet connection.
03 Concept C

Basic Electronic Logic-Gate System

  • Simple electronic circuits detect faults and send signals which are processed through logic gates.
  • The outputs trigger warning lights or alarms to display the information.
