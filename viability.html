03 Risk & Mitigation

What could go wrong,
and what holds it.

A working register of the risks identified across the project — technical, community, operational, and financial — with the likelihood, severity, and the mitigation plan keeping each one in check. Filter by category to focus on a specific area.

Low Medium High
Technical
Likelihood Medium Severity High

GSM signal dropout in remote areas

Risk

SMS alerts fail to dispatch when cellular coverage is weak or absent across parts of Cape York.

Mitigation

External antenna, message retry queue with timestamping, dashboard log retained on-device, and quarterly signal-survey of installation sites.

Technical
Likelihood Medium Severity High

Enclosure failure under wet-season conditions

Risk

Water ingress or UV degradation compromises internal hardware during extended monsoon periods.

Mitigation

ABS shell + TPU gasket tested across heat, water, dust, vibration; IP-rated cable glands; pole-mount above flood line; six-month inspection cycle.

Technical
Likelihood Low Severity Medium

False positive fault alerts

Risk

Edge readings near threshold limits generate alerts that don't reflect real faults, eroding trust in the system.

Mitigation

Hysteresis bands on thresholds; alerts require sustained breach (≥2 cycles); thresholds tunable post-deployment based on field data.

Community
Likelihood Low Severity High

Cultural protocol misalignment

Risk

Installation sites or maintenance activities inadvertently affect culturally significant areas.

Mitigation

Site approval through Lama Lama Land Trust and ranger leadership before each install; Aboriginal Cultural Heritage Act 2003 duty-of-care guidelines followed throughout.

Community
Likelihood Medium Severity Medium

Reliance on external technical support

Risk

System repairs or threshold adjustments require external technicians, reducing community autonomy.

Mitigation

Plain-language maintenance guide for rangers; standard tooling only (no proprietary fasteners); modular components for field-swap; training built into rollout.

Operational
Likelihood High Severity Medium

Wet-season access delays

Risk

Road closures during wet season prevent on-site maintenance for weeks at a time.

Mitigation

Pre-wet-season inspection sweep; spare components stocked locally with rangers; remote diagnostics via SMS keep operators informed during isolation.

Operational
Likelihood Medium Severity Medium

Wildlife interference

Risk

Insects, rodents, or larger fauna damage cabling or block sensor inlets.

Mitigation

Sealed cable glands; pole-mount placement raises unit above ground-level fauna; mesh-protected ventilation; visual inspection at every site visit.

Financial
Likelihood Medium Severity High

Grant funding shortfall

Risk

AEA Ignite outcome or industry partner co-investment falls short of projected scale-up budget.

Mitigation

Multi-source funding strategy: ARENA Regional Microgrids Program as secondary path; reduced-scope deployment plan with two-unit minimum viable rollout.

Financial
Likelihood Low Severity Medium

Ongoing service-fee creep

Risk

ThingsBoard / Twilio pricing changes make the monthly recurring cost untenable for the community.

Mitigation

Self-hosted ThingsBoard Community Edition as fallback; SMS-only mode that bypasses dashboard infrastructure; annual cost review built into IGC governance.
