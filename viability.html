03 Funding Pathway

Funding Timeline · AEA to IGC.

Running parallel to the implementation rollout is a funding pathway that moves the project from concept proposal through to long-term, community-led operation. Each phase secures the next, building toward the formation of an Indigenous Governance Committee (IGC) that takes ownership of the system.
